At TechBox by Gretta, we offer a range of programs designed to equip tech talent and entrepreneurs with the skills, mentorship, and resources they need to succeed. Whether you’re looking to learn new skills, launch a startup, or grow within the tech industry, our tailored programs provide a pathway to success.

Mission:

To equip African tech talent with the skills, resources, and networks they need to innovate, lead, and succeed in the global tech industry.

Vision:

To be Africa’s leading hub for tech education, startup incubation, and innovation, fostering a thriving tech ecosystem across the continent.

Our Story

TechBox was born at Gretta out of a desire to close the skills gap and foster innovation in Africa’s tech sector. We noticed a growing demand for skilled tech professionals and a lack of opportunities for budding entrepreneurs to bring their ideas to life. By combining hands-on tech training, startup support, and community building, we’re creating a comprehensive ecosystem that empowers tech talent at every stage of their journey.

Since our inception, we’ve helped thousands of individuals gain valuable skills, supported dozens of startups, and cultivated a vibrant community of tech professionals who are shaping the future of Africa's tech landscape.
