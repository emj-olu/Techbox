Policies

1. Introduction

Welcome to TechBox by Gretta. This Policy Page outlines the various policies that govern your use of our website and services. By accessing our site and participating in our programs, you agree to comply with these policies.

2. Enrollment Policy

  • Eligibility: All participants must meet the eligibility criteria for specific programs as stated on their individual registration pages.
  • Registration: Participants must complete the registration process and provide accurate information to enroll in programs.
  • Payment: Program fees must be paid in full before the start date unless stated otherwise. Payment methods accepted include credit/debit cards and other specified methods.

3. Cancellation and Refund Policy

  • Cancellation: Participants may cancel their enrollment up to 14 days before the program start date for a full refund. Cancellations made after this period may not be eligible for a refund.
  • Refunds: Refunds will be processed within 3 days of receiving the cancellation request. The refund will be issued through the original payment method used for registration.

4. Code of Conduct

Participants are expected to maintain professionalism and respect toward instructors, fellow participants, and TechBox staff. Any form of harassment, discrimination, or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated and may result in removal from the program without a refund.

5. Privacy and Data Protection

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how TechBox by Gretta collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

  • Information We Collect: We may collect personal information from you when you register for our programs, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us. This information may include Name, Email address, Phone number, and any other information you provide voluntarily.
  • How We Use Your Information: We use your information to:
    • Provide and manage our services
    • Communicate with you regarding your inquiries and registrations
    • Improve our website and services
    • Send periodic emails about new programs, updates, or other information
  • Data Protection: We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access or loss.
  • Sharing Your Information: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties without your consent, except as required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Third-Party Services: Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We do not control these sites and are not responsible for their content or privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies before providing any personal information.

6. Intellectual Property Policy

All course materials, content, and resources provided by TechBox by Gretta are the property of TechBox or its licensors. Participants are prohibited from reproducing, distributing, or modifying these materials without prior written permission.

7. Changes to Policies

TechBox by Gretta reserves the right to modify these policies at any time. Participants will be notified of any significant changes via email or through notices on our website.

8. Contact Us

For questions or concerns regarding our policies, please contact us at: info@techbox.ng
