Frequently
Asked Questions

Q1: What is TechBox by Gretta?

TechBox by Gretta is a platform designed to provide aspiring tech professionals with training, mentorship, and resources to accelerate their careers in technology.

Q1: What is TechBox by Gretta?

TechBox by Gretta is a tech ecosystem providing hands-on training, startup support, and a thriving tech community. We focus on equipping individuals with in-demand skills and supporting startups across Africa to bring their ideas to life.

Q2: Who can join TechBox programs?

TechBox programs are open to anyone passionate about tech, whether you're a beginner looking to learn new skills, an experienced professional seeking to expand your knowledge, or a startup founder in need of support.

Q3: How long do the programs last?

Each TechBox program varies in length. Our training bootcamps typically run between 8 to 12 weeks, while the startup incubator program can last from 3 to 6 months, depending on the specific track.

Q4: What kind of support does TechBox provide for startups?

TechBox offers mentorship from industry experts, access to tech talent, resources, and networking opportunities. Our incubator helps early-stage startups refine their ideas and build sustainable businesses.

Q5: Are there any prerequisites for joining a program?

For most of our programs, no prior experience is required, especially for beginners. However, some advanced tracks may require foundational knowledge in specific tech skills.
