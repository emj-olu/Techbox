At TechBox by Gretta, we offer a range of programs designed to equip tech talent and entrepreneurs with the skills, mentorship, and resources they need to succeed. Whether you’re looking to learn new skills, launch a startup, or grow within the tech industry, our tailored programs provide a pathway to success.

Our Programs

1.

Tech Talent
Accelerator

An intensive, hands-on program focused on teaching high-demand tech skills like web development, UX/UI design, cybersecurity, data analytics and more. With real-world projects, mentorship, and career support, this accelerator helps you build a portfolio and gain job-ready skills.

  • 8-12 Weeks Bootcamp.
  • Real-World Tech Projects.
  • Mentorship from industry experts.
  • Career support and job placement assistance.
Who is it for?

Aspiring developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts looking to break into the tech industry.

2.

Startup
Incubator

Our incubator program provides early-stage startups with mentorship, resources, and a pool of tech talent to bring their ideas to life. Over the course of 3-6 months, startups receive personalized support to refine their products, gain market insight, and develop MVPs.

  • Personalized mentorship from industry veterans.
  • Access to tech talent for collaboration.
  • Funding and resource support for prototype/MVP development.
Who is it for?

Early-stage tech startups with innovative ideas looking for the right support to grow

3.

Innovation
Lab

The Innovation Lab provides a space for tech innovators, developers, and designers to come together and work on solutions to real-world challenges. Partnering with industry leaders and external clients, participants gain practical experience while making an impact.

  • Collaborative project cycles with industry partners.
  • Opportunities to solve real-world challenges.
  • Networking and professional exposure.
Who is it for?

Developers, tech professionals, and innovators looking to work on impactful projects.

4.

Career Pathway
Program

This 6-month program offers career coaching, job placement assistance, and access to a network of tech employers. Designed to help tech professionals take their careers to the next level, the program offers personal development and career planning resources.

  • One-on-one career coaching sessions.
  • Job placement assistance.
  • Access to exclusive job opportunities.
Who is it for?

Tech professionals at any stage of their career seeking guidance and new opportunities.

Why Choose TechBox?

  • Hands-On Learning: Our programs emphasize real-world applications and provide practical experience through projects and mentorship.
  • Expert Mentorship: Gain insights and guidance from industry experts who are actively shaping the tech world.
  • Comprehensive Support: We offer end-to-end support from learning tech skills to launching startups, ensuring success at every stage.

Ready to level up your tech career or launch your startup? Explore our programs and find the one that’s right for you.
